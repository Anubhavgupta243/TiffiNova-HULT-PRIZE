# Tiffinova 🍱
Bridging the gap between students and quality nutrition through innovation and strategic logistics.

🚀 The Vision
Student nutrition is frequently the first casualty of a busy academic life. Tiffinova was born out of a two-year journey to solve this crisis. Our mission is to connect students with licensed food providers, ensuring that high standards of hygiene, quality, and affordability are never compromised.

By exploring strategic collaborations with established giants like the Akshaya Patra Foundation, Tiffinova aims to create a scalable, high-impact model that treats student health as a priority, not an afterthought.

🏆 The Journey: Hult Prize 2025
Tiffinova isn't just an idea; it’s a validated solution.

2024: Participants in the Hult Prize.

2025: On-Campus Winners, securing the top spot and a prize of ₹3,000.

Despite winning the on-campus round of this international competition, we encountered a classic startup "roadblock": a lack of institutional funding to reach the national level. However, we believe innovation doesn’t stop when a door is closed—it just recalibrates. We are moving forward with a winning pitch and a solid growth roadmap.

🛠️ Core Strategy & Features
Quality Assurance: Partnering exclusively with licensed, vetted food providers.

Affordability: Optimizing the supply chain to fit student budgets.

Strategic Logistics: A business model designed for urban campus density.

Resilient Growth: A comprehensive debt-restructuring and scalability roadmap.
